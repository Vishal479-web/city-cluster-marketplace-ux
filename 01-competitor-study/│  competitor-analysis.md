## Competitors Studied
1. Toptal
2. Upwork
3. Fiverr Business

### Key Observations (8–12 bullets)

- **Toptal**
  - First CTA: “Hire Top Talent”
  - Trust via heavy vetting messaging and case studies
  - Choice reduction through expert matching instead of browsing
  - Steal: Strong vetting narrative
  - Avoid: Overly long onboarding

- **Upwork**
  - First CTA: “Post a Job”
  - Trust via ratings, earnings, and work history
  - Choice overload with too many profiles
  - Steal: Clear pricing transparency
  - Avoid: Marketplace clutter

- **Fiverr Business**
  - First CTA: “Find Talent”
  - Trust via curated business talent
  - Reduced choices via pre-packaged services
  - Steal: Service-based framing
  - Avoid: Limited collaboration visibility
